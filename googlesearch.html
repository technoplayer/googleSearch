    Musa velutina, the hairy banana or pink banana, is a diploid species of wild banana. These plants are originally from Assam and the eastern Himalayas. The fruits are 3 in long, pink, and fuzzy. They are borne on erect flower stalks with a pink inflorescence. Wikipedia
    Scientific name : Musa velutina
    Higher classification : Musa
    Rank : Species
    Family : Musaceae
